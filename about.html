About Us!

Who We Are

Are you a nonprofit in need of furniture, office supplies, or other items? Do you have something a nonprofit could use? Search for items needed and available here. Anyone can post free items.

The Community Foundation of Anne Arundel County (CFAAC) is one of the largest funders of nonprofit organizations in Anne Arundel County. CFAAC distributes nearly $1.5 million in grants each year to a variety of nonprofit organizations. We are a tax exempt, 501(c)(3), publicly supported philanthropic organization with the long term goal of building permanent funds that provide support to local nonprofit organizations through grants and special projects. Our mission is to inspire and promote giving in Anne Arundel County by connecting people who care with causes that matter.
